ROLE ACCESS MATRIX V1
================================================================================

Roles:
- Admin
- Trustee
- Viewer

GENERAL EXPECTATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Admin:
- full access across all modules
- create/edit access where applicable

Trustee:
- access to operational modules
- create/edit access for workspace, discussions, decisions, tasks, documents
- limited admin-only content management restrictions remain

Viewer:
- read access only where intended
- no create/edit actions on protected operational pages
