# Trust Tax Assistant Direction Confirmed

## Confirmed Direction
The system is intended to function as a fiduciary and trust tax assistant, not merely a trust management app.

## Important Scope
The product should support tax-ready data preparation, accrual-aware accounting logic, and form-mapping structures for trust administration and trust tax workflows.

## Core Principle
The system should assist with tax purposes by organizing, calculating, and preparing data needed for common trust tax forms and fiduciary filings, while remaining a preparation and intelligence system rather than an automatic filing engine.

## Confirmed Build Direction
Proceed with the accrual-aware accounting layer before jumping directly into IRS form workflows.

## Why This Order Matters
A stable accounting and accrual engine is required before reliable trust tax preparation logic can be built.

## Expected Future Support Areas
- cash vs accrual accounting views
- receivables / payables style tracking
- trust financial summaries
- tax-ready categorized data
- IRS trust form preparation structures
- fiduciary filing support workflows
