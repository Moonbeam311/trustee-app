# Accrual Accounting Layer Complete

## What Was Added
The system now includes an accrual-aware accounting layer on top of the persistent fiduciary ledger.

## Current Capabilities
- Chart of accounts seeding per trust
- Ledger entry classification
- Accounting method awareness (cash / accrual)
- Recognition date support
- Due date support
- Paid date support
- Chart account tagging on entries
- Trust-level financial summary view
- Cash income / expense / net calculations
- Accrual income / expense / net calculations
- Receivables and payables summary logic

## Product Meaning
The product now includes the accounting foundation needed to support tax-assistant and trust-tax-preparation workflows.

## Strategic Direction
This confirms that the system is evolving into a fiduciary + tax intelligence platform rather than remaining only a trust administration app.
