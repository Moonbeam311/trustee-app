# Date Intelligence Complete

## What Was Added
The asset intelligence layer now interprets date fields rather than only storing them.

## Current Capabilities
- Expiring within 30 days
- Expiring within 60 days
- Expiring within 90 days
- Review due within 30 days
- Review due within 60 days
- Review due within 90 days
- Missing custodian
- Missing review date
- Orphaned assets

## Product Meaning
The system is moving from passive record storage into fiduciary intelligence and oversight support.

## Strategic Direction
This strengthens the platform as a trustee-first and fiduciary-first wealth management operating system that can identify risk, missing governance, and upcoming deadlines.
