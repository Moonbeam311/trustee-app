# Severity Layer Complete

## What Was Added
The asset intelligence layer now includes overdue detection and severity scoring.

## Current Capabilities
- Expired assets detection
- Overdue review detection
- Severity scoring with levels:
  - LOW
  - MEDIUM
  - HIGH
  - CRITICAL
- Severity reasons displayed alongside each asset
- Existing due-soon and missing-governance logic retained

## Product Meaning
The system is now moving from date awareness into prioritization and risk signaling. It is beginning to behave like a fiduciary decision engine rather than a passive record store.

## Strategic Direction
This strengthens the product as a trustee-first and fiduciary-first wealth management operating system capable of signaling urgency and governance failures.
