# Wealth System Option A Roadmap

## Confirmed Direction
The product is being positioned as a trust-centered wealth management and fiduciary operating system.

## Option A Priority Order
1. Finish durable persistence backbone
2. Expand property into full asset mapping
3. Add dates, deadlines, and responsibility fields
4. Refine draft/final lifecycle
5. Move into generated packet/reporting output

## Why This Matters
The system should not be limited to real property. It must support broader wealth and control mapping, including:
- real property
- personal tangible property
- financial property
- business interests
- intellectual property
- digital property
- contractual rights

## Important Examples
### Intellectual Property
- STEM Nation
- recipes
- curriculum
- codebase
- branding

### Digital Property
- websites
- domains
- hosting accounts
- repositories
- app assets

## Core System Principle
This product should function as a trustee-first and fiduciary-first operating system, not merely a portfolio viewer or legal form tool.

## Immediate Next Build Step
Ledger persistence

## After Ledger Persistence
- asset mapping expansion
- date logic
- responsibility mapping
- tasks/reminders
- reporting and generated packet output
