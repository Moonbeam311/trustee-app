# Tax Assistant Phase 1 Complete

## What Was Added
A stable trust tax assistant page was added to the system.

## Current Capabilities
- Tax Assistant route: /tax_assistant
- Trust selector for analysis
- Trust tax overview display
- Applicable form guidance including:
  - Form 1041
  - Schedule K-1
  - Form 1041-ES
  - Form 56
- Tax readiness issue detection
- Integration with existing trust, ledger, and accounting-method data

## Product Meaning
The platform now includes the beginning of a tax-preparation intelligence layer on top of the fiduciary accounting system.

## Strategic Direction
This confirms the system is evolving into a fiduciary + tax intelligence platform capable of helping organize and prepare trust administration data for tax purposes.
