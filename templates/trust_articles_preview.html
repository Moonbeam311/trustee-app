ARTICLES OF TRUST These Articles of Trust are adopted and declared for the establishment and administration of {{ trust.trust_name or "[Trust Name]" }}. ARTICLE I — NAME The name of the Trust shall be: {{ trust.trust_name or "[Trust Name]" }} ARTICLE II — DATE OF FORMATION The Trust is established effective: {{ trust.created_at or "[Effective Date]" }} ARTICLE III — NATURE OF TRUST The Trust shall operate as a private trust arrangement under its governing trust instrument and related trust records, subject to its stated purposes, trustee powers, and governing law. ARTICLE IV — PURPOSE The purpose of the Trust is to hold, manage, protect, and administer trust property and related trust records. ARTICLE V — PRINCIPAL PLACE OF ADMINISTRATION The principal place of administration shall be the address reflected in the governing trust records, or such other place as the Trustee(s) may determine. ARTICLE VI — SETTLOR / GRANTOR The Settlor / Grantor of the Trust is: {{ trust.grantor_name or "[Grantor / Settlor]" }} ARTICLE VII — TRUSTEE(S) The acting Trustee(s) shall serve according to the governing trust instrument and related appointments, acceptances, resignations, removals, or successor designations. ARTICLE VIII — TRUST PROPERTY The Trust Estate shall consist of all property now or later assigned, transferred, conveyed, exchanged, scheduled, gifted, or otherwise administered as trust property under the Trust. ARTICLE IX — TRUST ADMINISTRATION RECORDS The Trust may maintain records including declarations, certifications, schedules, assignments, trustee acceptances, minutes, and related administration records. ARTICLE X — LIMITATION These Articles of Trust are intended as a summary organizational instrument and do not replace the full governing trust instrument.
