Universal Instruction Framework

  1. Identify the asset with enough specificity to distinguish it from unrelated property.
  2. Confirm the intended transfer type and packet mode.
  3. Record originating assignment language before trustee-level acceptance.
  4. Document trustee review and trustee decision clearly.
  5. Record what changed in reality, not only what changed on paper.
  6. Generate supporting records, including Schedule A, transfer log, and minutes.
  7. Review the packet for completeness before finalizing.
  8. After finalization, treat the packet as read-only unless formally reopened by a later process.
