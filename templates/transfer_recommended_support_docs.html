When Recommended Support Docs Should Usually Be Present

  1. When the packet is intended to serve as a reusable record beyond a one-time internal draft.
  2. When the transfer may later be reviewed by another trustee, fiduciary, or administrator.
  3. When control evidence is not self-explanatory from the base packet alone.
  4. When packet clarity and long-term audit readability are important.
  5. When the user wants a more complete and standardized transfer file.
