Document / Intangible Rights Support Categories

  • Rights-description clarification note
  • Document chain or source-reference note
  • Control/possession explanation for non-physical assets
  • Supporting note for assignment of record-based rights
  • Index of related documents or references
  • Custody/location note for original document storage
  • Document-specific packet cover note

When Document / Intangible Rights Support Docs May Help

  1. When the asset is primarily documentary, contractual, or record-based rather than physical.
  2. When the control story depends on who holds, records, or administers the document or right.
  3. When the packet should explain how the right is identified and linked to source records.
  4. When later reviewers may need extra context to understand what was transferred.
  5. When original-document custody or reference integrity matters.
