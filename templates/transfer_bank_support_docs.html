When Bank / Account Support Docs May Help

  1. When a financial institution may need clearer packet-facing instructions.
  2. When account control changes depend on signer, authority, or title updates.
  3. When the transfer needs a better audit trail for external follow-up.
  4. When the asset is intangible and not obvious from possession alone.
  5. When communication with a bank or account custodian needs to be documented.
