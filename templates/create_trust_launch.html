Create Trust Launch

Begin a controlled trust creation workflow. This launch screen does not replace the existing wizard. It simply explains the path before entering Step 1, so the current variable carryover and final document process remain intact.

Controlled Creation Flow

The existing wizard will create the trust record first, then carry the entered values through each step into the Formation Preview Hub and final document surfaces.

  1. Step 1: Trust identity, name, jurisdiction, effective date, and starting record.
  2. Step 2: Grantor / settlor details.
  3. Step 3: Trust structure, purpose, accounting method, and workflow mode.
  4. Step 4: Trustee, successor trustee, and beneficiary structure.
  5. Step 5: Governance, recordkeeping, and administration preferences.
  6. Step 6: Trust property, corpus, accounts, and asset category details.
  7. Finalize: Review and finalize into the Formation Preview Hub.
