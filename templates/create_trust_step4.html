Create Trust Wizard — Step 4: Administration Preferences

What is this?
This step sets the default operating preferences for how the trust will be handled inside the app.
Why this matters
These choices shape how records are classified, how guidance appears, and how the administration workflow behaves later.
What do these mean?
Private: intended for limited internal trust records and sensitive administration details.
Internal: for normal operating records within the trust office environment.
Public-facing: for records likely to be used in outward-facing, filing, or presentation contexts.
Why are we asking this again?
This confirms whether the trust is being organized mainly for internal/private administration or for workflows that may include outward-facing records.
What is this?
When enabled, the system will explain terms, fields, and common next steps throughout the workflow.
What is this?
When enabled, the system will recommend related administrative steps, records, and document categories based on the trust configuration.
